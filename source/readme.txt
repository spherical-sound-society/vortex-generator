Three firmwares available:
Rev 2.023 is the official one
Rev 2.024 is one that the envelopes are shorter and faster, nice to FM other things
Turing is a kind of turing machine implementation, where SHFT outputs 16 steps sequences. Channel A is the clock of the sequencer and moving ATTACK B channel creates a brand new random sequence 


To compile code you need to install this library:

https://github.com/MajenkoLibraries/MCPDAC
